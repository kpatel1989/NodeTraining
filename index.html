Welcome to Pin notes.

Pin-notes is a virtual pin board which helps you to pin your important notes right on your desktop. You can create as many notes you want. You can even create groups where you can share your notes. Your personal and group notes are kept separate. You can add as many friends you want to a group. they all will be able to see you shared notes. The group boards are updated as soon as some one adds or updates or deletes a note from the group.
